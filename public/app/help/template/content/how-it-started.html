How It Started

In 2014, data on resource utilization was collected and analyzed. As a result of the analysis, there was a need to facilitate an increase in utilization. The aim was to develop a platform where employees can conveniently access a pool of opportunities that supported skill and career development. The platform will drive employee engagement, aid in retaining key talent, and increase utilization.

To address this need, was developed in 2015. opportunities@HSBC serves as a systematic approach for employees to share developmental opportunities and experiences for other employees to participate in. Employees will be able to fill up their under-utilized time when their project work slows down. Alternatively, they can explore what is out there outside of their current roles. Using next generation technology, the tool was designed to match opportunity seekers to opportunity owners based on skill sets and interests.

Some of the outcomes include the following networking benefits:

  • Employees can obtain additional input that are valuable to their project deliverables
  • Employees can enhance current skills and expand their professional development
