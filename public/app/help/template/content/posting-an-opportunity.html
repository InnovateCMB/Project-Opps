Posting an Opportunity

How can I post an opportunity?

To mark yourself as not actively seeking for opportunities, proceed with these steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “New Project” button to launch the Create A New Project Page.
  • In the Create A New Project Page, input the opportunity details.
  • Once you are finished inputting the opportunity details, click on “Submit” button.
  • Click on “OK” button in confirmation modal window to launch the Opportunity Details Page.

How do I locate seekers who have a specific skill?

To locate seekers who have a specify skill, proceed with these steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “My Profile” link to launch your Profile Page.
  • In your Profile Page, click on “My Dashboard” to launch a dropdown menu.
  • In the dropdown menu, click on “All Users”.
  • In the All Users Page, input skill into “Search for name, skill, role, location, email…” field then click on search button.
  • In the All Users Page, all the users who have the skill you are searching for will be displayed.
