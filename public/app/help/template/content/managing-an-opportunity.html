Managing an Opportunity

How do I edit the details of an opportunity I have posted?

To edit the details of an opportunity you have posted, proceed with these steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “My Profile” link to launch your Profile Page.
  • In your Profile Page, go to “Current Projects” tab.
  • In “Current Projects” tab, locate the opportunity you have posted and click on “View Details” button to launch the owner-view of the Opportunity Details Page.
  • In the owner-view of the Opportunity Details Page, click on “Edit” button to launch the Edit The Project Page.
  • Once you are finished editing the opportunity details, click on “Submit” button.

How do I view the seekers who have applied for an opportunity I have posted?

To view the seekers who have applied for an opportunity you have posted, proceed with the following steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “My Profile” link to launch your Profile Page.
  • In your Profile Page, go to “Current Projects” tab.
  • In “Current Projects” tab, locate the opportunity you have posted and click on “View Details” button to launch the owner-view of the Opportunity Details Page.
  • In the owner-view of the Opportunity Details Page, go to “Applied People” tab to view the seekers who have applied to your opportunity.

How do I select an applicant to work on an opportunity I have posted?

To select an applicant to work on an opportunity you have posted, proceed with the following steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “My Profile” link to launch your Profile Page.
  • In your Profile Page, go to “Current Projects” tab.
  • In “Current Projects” tab, locate the opportunity you have posted and click on “View Details” button to launch the owner-view of the Opportunity Details Page.
  • In the owner-view of the Opportunity Details Page, go to “Applied People” tab to view the seekers who have applied to your opportunity.
  • In “Applied People” tab, click on “Accept” button next to the name of the applicant you would like to select.
  • Click on “OK” button in confirmation modal window.
  • The name of the applicant you have selected will be displayed in “Selected People” tab. The same applicant will no longer appear in “Applied People” tab.
