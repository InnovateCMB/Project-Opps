Opportunity Seekers

Who are opportunity seekers?

Are you looking to learn something new and grow your current skills? In addition to your current assignments, are you looking to stretch yourself by engaging in an extra project? As an opportunity seeker, you can network with other channels and lines of business by:

  • Browsing through an archive of opportunities
  • Asking questions about opportunities that you are interested in
  • Applying for opportunities
  • Exploring and collaborating after onboarding onto an opportunity

How it works for opportunity seekers

Step 1. Seek

Looking to learn something new and grow your skills? Seek for an opportunity.

Step 2. Apply

Found an opportunity you are interested in? Apply for it.

Step 3. Collaborate

Once onboarded to a project, explore, and collaborate.
