Opportunity Owners

Who are opportunity owners?

Are you involved in a project or cause that can benefit from additional ideas and extra helping hands? As an opportunity owner, you can network with resources by:

  • Posting an opportunity to spread the word about the expertise you are looking for
  • Responding to queries from seekers who are interested in your opportunity
  • Reviewing profiles of applicants to your opportunity
  • Reviewing profiles of potential seekers who may have the expertise you are looking
  • Onboarding seekers to assist with your opportunity

How it works for opportunity owners

Step 1. Announce

Need more ideas and extra help for a project? Post an opportunity and spread the word to seekers.

Step 2. Review

Review the profiles of the applicants and potential seekers who may have the expertise you are looking for.

Step 3. Select

Onboard a seeker to the project!
