Applying for an Opportunity

What types of opportunities will I have access to?

Seekers across the group will be able to access opportunities marked as “GLOBAL”. You will also be able to access opportunities marked as “LOCAL” if it is posted by an opportunity owner from the same country as you.

How do I apply for an opportunity?

To apply for an opportunity, click on “Apply” button in the seeker-view of the Opportunity Details Page.
