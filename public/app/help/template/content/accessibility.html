Accessibility

What are the technical requirements for accessing opportunities@HSBC?

The opportunities@HSBC platform is optimized for Internet Explorer 11 on Windows 7, Chrome and Mozilla Firefox.

Do I need to sign up for an account?

As long as you are an employee of HSBC and have a PeopleSoft ID, then you will be able to access opportunities@HSBC. A single sign on system is in place. When you visit the opportunities@HSBC Home Page for the first time, your account will be created automatically. You will not have to go through a separate process for signing up.
