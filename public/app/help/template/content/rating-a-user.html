Rating a User

How do I rate a user?

To mark yourself as not actively seeking for opportunities, proceed with these steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “New Project” button to launch the Create A New Project Page.
  • In the Create A New Project Page, input the opportunity details.
  • Once you are finished inputting the opportunity details, click on “Submit” button.
  • Click on “OK” button in confirmation modal window to launch the Opportunity Details Page./li>
