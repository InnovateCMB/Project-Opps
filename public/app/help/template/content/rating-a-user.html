Rating a User

How do I rate an opportunity owner?

After a seeker is selected to work on an opportunity, the seeker can rate the owner. If you are a seeker looking to rate an owner, proceed with the following steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “My Profile” link to launch your Profile Page.
  • In your Profile Page, go to “Current Projects” tab.
  • In “Current Projects” tab, locate the opportunity you have posted and click on “View Details” button to launch the seeker-view of the Opportunity Details Page.
  • In the seeker-view of the Opportunity Details Page, hover over the stars next to “Rate this project’s owner”. For example, if you would like to provide a 3-star rating to the owner, hover over 3 stars.

How do I rate an opportunity seeker?

After a seeker is selected to work on an opportunity, the owner can rate the seeker. If you are an owner looking to rate a seeker, proceed with the following steps:
  • Go to the Home Page.
  • In the Home Page, click on “My Profile” link to launch your Profile Page.
  • In your Profile Page, go to “Current Projects” tab.
  • In “Current Projects” tab, locate the opportunity you have posted and click on “View Details” button to launch the seeker-view of the Opportunity Details Page.
  • In the seeker-view of the Opportunity Details Page, go to “Selected People” tab and locate the seeker you would like to rate.
  • In “Selected People” tab, hover over the stars next to the name of the seeker you would like to rate. For example, if you would like to provide a 3-star rating to the seeker, hover over 3 stars.
